My Favorite Hobby:
Cooking

My favorite hobby is cooking. When we are free, me and my mom will head to the kitchen to go whip up some snacks.
Learning and developing food skills is very important. If there is no one to cook for you, and you can't get takeout, what will you do?
Well, that's where the cooking comes in. If you can create incredible dishes, your guests will be amazed! And cooking is an awesome career, too!
If you decide to become a culinary artist, you may get the chance to get on TV, and cook for famous personalities. You may even become one yourself!
That's why I like to cook. The next time you're in your kitchen, try making an exquisite meal. Who knows? Maybe you'll become a Michelin Star award-winning chef!
